# ⚖️ The Fine Print: AI Legal Risk Assessor

**"Don't sign what you don't understand."**

This tool uses AI to scan legal documents (PDFs & Text), detect predatory clauses like hidden fees or privacy waivers, and translate them into plain English.

![App Screenshot](screenshot.png) 
*(Add a screenshot of your app here)*

## 🚀 Features
* **Drag-and-Drop:** Upload PDF contracts or .txt files.
* **Risk Detection:** Flags high-risk clauses (Arbitration, Privacy, Fees).
* **AI Explainer:** Translates legal jargon into simple terms using Google's Flan-T5.
* **Batch Processing:** Scans long documents instantly.
* **Export:** Download a CSV report of all findings.

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
* **Python** (Logic)
* **Streamlit** (UI Dashboard)
* **Hugging Face Transformers** (AI Models)
* **PyPDF** (Document Parsing)
